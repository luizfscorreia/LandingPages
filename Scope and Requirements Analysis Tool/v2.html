Enabling SAP's Clean Core Approach What is

Clean Core?

Clean Core is SAP's strategic framework for building and maintaining ERP systems that are agile, upgrade-stable, and innovation-ready. It's not about eliminating customizations entirely‚Äîit's about ensuring that any extension or customization is properly decoupled from the core, maintaining upgrade stability and enabling continuous business transformation.

Why Clean Core Matters for SuccessFactors

Organizations with Clean Core implementations can:

  • Accelerate innovation cycles - Adopt new SAP features without disruption
  • Reduce upgrade complexity - Maintain stable upgrade paths with decoupled extensions
  • Unlock incremental business value - Up to 75% more value over time according to SAP research
  • Lower total cost of ownership - Reduce technical debt and maintenance burden
  • Stay competitive - Quickly adapt to market changes with agile, flexible systems
